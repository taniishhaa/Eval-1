Event Description

The Alpha Music Festival is an open-air music festival in Chandigarh, India that features a lineup of artists, pop-ups, and a variety of attractions. The festival is known for its infectious beats and wild vibes. Alpha Music Festival 4.0: This festival is scheduled for August 31, 2024 at the Forest Hill Resort.
